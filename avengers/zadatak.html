infinity stones

Power stone
Increased strength and durability, enhances virtually any known superhuman ability, energy manipulation.
Space stone
Travel through space, mostly through teleportation.
Reality stone
Alters all of reality.
Soul stone
Observe, attack or even steal a being's soul or spirit.
Time stone
Time travel; stop, slow down or speed up flow of time.
Mind stone
Near-limitless psionic/psychic abilities including empathy, telepathy and telekinesis.
